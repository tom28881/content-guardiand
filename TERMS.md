## 3. App Description

Content Guardian is a Confluence Cloud application that:
- Scans Confluence pages to detect stale, inactive, orphaned, and incomplete content
- Provides a dashboard for reviewing and managing problematic pages
- Allows bulk actions (archive, whitelist, tag) on detected pages
- Maintains an audit log of all actions performed
